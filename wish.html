You step into a new year.

You carry warmth in quiet ways — in how you notice things, in how you stay gentle even when the world isn’t.

You deserve mornings that feel lighter, evenings that feel understood, and moments where you smile without trying.

And if somewhere along the way, you find me walking beside you, not ahead, not behind — just there, then this year would already feel a little special.
— A soft beginning
